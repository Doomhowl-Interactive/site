Vortex

Our app, Vortex, lets users take a photo with their phone's camera or choose one from their gallery to use as the game background. This is done using Android intents, ensuring that only the images taken or selected through this process are accessible by the app. Doomhowl Interactive does not collect or store any user-generated images.
