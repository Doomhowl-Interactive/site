Blasteroids!

An arcade Asteroids shooter with multiple themes!

Shoot your way through the endless meteor storm in Blasteroids!, a free arcade asteroid shooter!
Get the highest score possible by filling your combo meter.

Features:

  • Features different themes to customize your experience.
  • Multiple difficulty options including 'Lunatic' mode, designed for the most skilled players!
  • Retro aesthetics with scanline filter.
  • Two control modes with customizable thumb stick configuration.
