Blasteroids v1.1.6 + Itch.io and Game Jolt release

Author: brambasiel | Date: 2023-09-23 | Game: Blasteroids!

No mobile version yet but, I’m excited to announce that Blasteroids! is now freely available on Itch.io and Game Jolt. This means it can reach a wider audience beyond the confines of my personal website. Additionally, the game now supports a fullscreen mode.

I’m now in the process of setting up my Google Play Console account to be able to release this game (and future ones) on mobile.

Please note that the mobile version of Blasteroids! will likely feature in-game advertisements because the Google Play Store is a highly competitive platform with lots of potential users. I’m curious to see the (lack of) profit that can be made. Just earning a few euros per month would already be nice.

Why put so much effort in releasing a small arcade game on different platforms?

The goal is to establish an efficient workflow, gain experience, and conduct research on different gaming marketplaces. This will improve the release process for future larger-scale games.
